About me

Accomplished robotics engineer with extensive experience in simulation, autonomous systems, and embedded software development. Demonstrated success in leading integration efforts on EU projects – including SMARTHANDLE, MERGING, and SHAREWORK – by developing high-fidelity digital twins and advanced control algorithms. Proficient in managing cross-functional teams to deliver innovative solutions in both academic and industrial environments, with a strong focus on technical excellence and leadership.

    Embedded Software Engineer at Paradox Kinetics

    Autonomous System Lead at UoP Racing Team

  • Jun 2020 - Present

    Senior Robotics Researcher at Laboratory for Manufacturing Systems & Automation (LMS)

    Lead integration efforts on EU projects such as SMARTHANDLE, MERGING, and SHAREWORK. Developed high-fidelity digital twins using NVIDIA Omniverse and Isaac Sim, designed robust embedded software architectures for robotic manipulation of flexible materials, and implemented advanced control algorithms—including a GPU-accelerated ROS package and a 14-DoF model-based planning algorithm.
